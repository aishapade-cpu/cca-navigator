Why I built this

I built CCA Navigator after losing a loved one to cholangiocarcinoma. After exhausting every option our doctors presented, we wished we'd had a better way to find and understand what else might be out there. CCA Navigator won't change the diagnosis — but it might help you walk into your next appointment knowing there's one more option on the table.

This is for every family still fighting.

— Aisha O., NYC
